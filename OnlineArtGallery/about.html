About ArtVault

We are the bridge between visionary artists and passionate art collectors worldwide.

Our Mission

Democratizing Fine Art

ArtVault was founded in 2020 with a single vision: make the world's finest art accessible to everyone, while giving artists the platform they deserve.

We connect artists directly with collectors, removing barriers and creating a transparent, fair marketplace for original art and limited-edition prints.
